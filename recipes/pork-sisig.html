Pork Sisig

Description

A popular Filipino dish made with chopped pork (traditionally pig’s face and ears), onions, chili peppers, and seasonings, usually served sizzling with a creamy, tangy flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb pork belly (or pork face, ears for authentic version)
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 2–3 chili peppers (sliced)
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp vinegar or calamansi juice
  • 1 tsp minced garlic
  • 2 tbsp mayonnaise
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 egg (optional, for serving)

Steps

  1. Boil and grill: Boil pork until tender, then grill or broil until crispy.
  2. Chop: Dice pork into small pieces.
  3. Sauté: In a hot pan, sauté garlic, onions, and chili. Add chopped pork.
  4. Season: Mix in soy sauce, vinegar, salt, and pepper.
  5. Finish: Stir in mayonnaise, adjust flavor.
  6. Serve: Place on a sizzling plate, optionally crack an egg on top before serving.

Tips for success

  • Use crispy grilled pork for authentic texture.
  • Adjust chili for spiciness level.
  • Add chicken liver for richer flavor (optional).
  • Serve hot on a sizzling plate with rice.
