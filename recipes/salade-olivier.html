Olivier salad

Description

A classic Russian potato salad made with diced boiled vegetables, eggs, pickles, peas, and cooked meat or sausage, all mixed with mayonnaise. It’s creamy, savory, and popular at festive gatherings.

Ingredients

  • 3 medium potatoes (boiled)
  • 2 medium carrots (boiled)
  • 3 hard-boiled eggs
  • 1 cup cooked chicken or bologna (diced)
  • 1 cup pickles (diced)
  • 1 cup canned peas (drained)
  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Boil potatoes, carrots, and eggs; cool and peel.
  2. Dice all ingredients (potatoes, carrots, eggs, pickles, meat).
  3. Combine in a large bowl with peas.
  4. Add mayonnaise, mix gently.
  5. Season with salt and pepper, chill before serving.

Tips for success

  • Dice ingredients evenly for better texture.
  • Use homemade or good-quality mayonnaise.
  • Chill for at least 1 hour for flavors to meld.
  • Adjust with sour cream for a lighter version.
